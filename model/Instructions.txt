Instructions for downloading pre-trained models:

1. Download VGG-19 from 'http://www.vlfeat.org/matconvnet/models/imagenet-vgg-verydeep-19.mat' and place it in 'models' directory as 'vgg19.model'.

2. Download VGG-16 from 'https://www.cs.toronto.edu/~frossard/vgg16/vgg16_weights.npz' and place it in 'models' directory as 'vgg16.model'.
